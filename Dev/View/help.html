How to Play

Hello and welcome to quiz game where you can test all of your trivial knowledge with up to 4 friends. To start, put yourown and your friends' names into the players page and press play for a general knowledge experience.

Difficulty and Categories

To test your knowledge in different categories, you can go to the "Game Settings" tab which gives you more categories to choose from as well as letting you select the difficulty. The default quizzes are general knowledge on a medium setting. If you want a challenge, set it to hard. If you want a fun, light-hearted quiz, pick easy.
